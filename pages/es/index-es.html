Our Story

GADEL Roosted Chicken !

Gabriel's journey began at a local roasted chicken restaurant. Inspired by loved ones, he turned his backyard into a takeout restaurant in 2019 to support his family and provide a quality education for his children. His dedication quickly resonated with the community, leading to a thriving business and dreams of a full-fledged restaurant.
